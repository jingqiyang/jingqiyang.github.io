Old School Stuff

  • TxtAd PL [video]
    Fall 2019
    TxtAd is a domain-specific language implemented in Haskell for developing text adventure games. TxtAd programs are compiled into text-based video games. TxtAd makes use of Template Haskell to perform analyses at compile-time to ensure games are well-formed.
    This was my passion project for Comp 150 Programming Language Design, my favorite course I've ever taken at Tufts. Check out the overview video linked in the title above!
    [GitHub Repo]
  • TA Simulator
    Fall 2018
    TA Simulator is a 2D video game made in Unity that teaches you how to TA computer science. Immerse yourself in the experience of being trapped in the claustrophobic Tufts CS Department building. Learn how to help your students in the various scenarios you may encounter in office hours!
    [GitHub Repo]
  • BiSpot
    JumboCode, Sept 2018 - May 2019
    BiSpot is a web application for the Bisexual Resource Center, an educational organization headquartered in Boston, MA. It displays a comprehensive map view of Bi+ organizations and events based on geographical locations, along with basic contact/sign-up guides.
    [GitHub Repo]
